# Hotel Sur Visual Style

Maintain the current prototype as dark, nebulous, slow, cinematic, and emotionally restrained.

## Core Mood

- Use a black-and-white base with low light and soft contrast.
- Use subtle orange `#f55033` only as an accent or highlight, never as a dominant wash.
- Favor foggy light, veils, analog grain, imperfect video texture, and slow drift.
- Keep movement organic, patient, and slightly unstable.
- Let the visual feel intimate and nocturnal, not spectacular.

## Avoid

- Do not make it EDM, clubby, neon, hyper-saturated, or arcade-like.
- Do not add audio bars, equalizer columns, flashy geometry, tunnels, lasers, or aggressive beat flashes.
- Do not over-sharpen, over-brighten, or make the image feel clean and digital.
- Do not introduce big UI chrome unless explicitly requested.

## Practical Checks

- Before changing visuals, identify whether the change supports the song mood.
- Keep pulses subtle and emotional rather than percussive.
- Prefer shadow, fog, and luma shifts over obvious shapes.
- Treat Leva controls as temporary exploration controls, not final product design.
